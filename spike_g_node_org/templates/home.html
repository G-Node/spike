Benchmark Datasets

What is this? Benchmark datasets consist of one or more trials of extracellular recordings. They are immutable and have known, yet undisclosed groundtruth.

What is it good for? A variety of datasets are provided for public download. You can immediately download one for yourself and apply your favorite spikesorting algorithm.

What to do next? We encourage you to come back and upload your results to contribute to the success of this platform.

I have a benchmark dataset! Very well! We are glad to host your benchmark dataset for public use on this platform. Please learn about the details in the documentation section.

Evaluation and Modules

What is this? When you upload your results, we will store and evaluate them against the groundtruth.

What about modules? The evaluation is a modular process. Each module provides a piece of evaluation and or visualisation of your uploaded results.

Will others see my results? All your evaluations will be private if you do not disclose them. Although, private evaluations do not factor into the public summaries!

I am missing a metric! We like to include every metric possible! You would need to provide a reference to a paper or an implementation of the metric.

I want to propose a module! Perfect! Same as for metrics, a reference or a sketch of plots will be needed.
